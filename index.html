About Us



Benchmark is an employee assesment platform that is powered by A.I used by recruiters/Employers to assess and weigh the level of skill and knowledge that a potential applicant has on the job they are applying for. The platform also analyse the level of proffesionalism potrayed by the applicant on the job they are apllying for.


    Some of the features of the platform are.


  • Virtual Interview about the role.
  • Applicant score card based on the realtime analysis collected from the interview.
  • Easy resume upload functionality for candidates to employer/recruiter.

Happy Clients -Our clients have been helping us test this platform dicreetly and adding input on emproving its functionality further.

Assesments -Powered by A.I in the backend enables the Benchmark platform to be able to perform over 100 different individual interviews all at ones using our A.I multithreading algorithms.

Years of experience -With over a decade of experience in the software development space, our team has worked tirelessly on this platform to ensure ease of work and a smooth and functional platform.
